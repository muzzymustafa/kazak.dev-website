MojiSpy

The Emoji Code-Breaking Game — Multiplayer, Competitive, Addictive.

MojiSpy is a fast-paced competitive puzzle game where you guess your opponent's secret emoji code before they crack yours. Play ranked matches, climb the leaderboard, and outsmart real opponents from around the world.

What Makes It Fun

  • Real-time PvP: Matched against live opponents with ELO-based ranking. Chess-clock time controls for Bullet, Blitz, and Classic modes.

Taskative: Team Tasks & Shifts

Stop Chaos. Start Managing Your Team's Daily Routines.

Taskative is the professional task manager designed for small businesses, field teams, and groups who need clarity without complexity. No confusing dashboards—just clean, fast, and reliable task management.

Why Teams Choose Taskative

  • Structured Team Management: Create groups, add your team members, and assign tasks instantly. You have full control over who joins your workspace—simple, secure, and organized.
  • Automate Work with Templates: Stop rewriting the same tasks every day. Use Reusable Templates for opening/closing checklists, SOPs, shift routines, maintenance tasks, or recurring operations.
  • Shared Shift & Task Calendar: Visualize all tasks and shifts in a clean calendar view. Instantly see who is working, what is due, and what's overdue.

Perfect For

Retail & Hospitality teams, Cleaning, HVAC, and maintenance crews, Logistics & field operations.

SustainMetrics: GHG Calculator & Emission Factor API

Accurate Data. Instant Calculations. Professional Reports.

SustainMetrics is a carbon accounting platform that simplifies Scope 1, 2, and 3 emission calculations using official government datasets. Free tier available — Pro and Business plans unlock cloud projects, API access, and advanced reporting.

What We Built

  • 4 Official Data Sources: UK DEFRA 2025, US EPA 2025, ADEME Base Carbone, and Ember global electricity data covering 193 countries.
  • Cloud Projects & Dashboard: Save inventories to the cloud, track emission trends over time, and manage multiple projects from a single dashboard.
  • PDF & Excel Export: Generate professional carbon reports in PDF or Excel format. Compare emission factors across sources and years.

Who It's For

Sustainability professionals, ESG consultants, environmental engineers, and organizations preparing carbon footprint reports.
