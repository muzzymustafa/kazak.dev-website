Taskative: Team Tasks & Shifts

Stop Chaos. Start Managing Your Team’s Daily Routines.

Taskative is the professional task manager designed for small businesses, field teams, and groups who need clarity without complexity. No confusing dashboards—just clean, fast, and reliable task management.

Why Teams Choose Taskative

  • Structured Team Management: Create groups, add your team members, and assign tasks instantly. You have full control over who joins your workspace—simple, secure, and organized.
  • Automate Work with Templates: Stop rewriting the same tasks every day. Use Reusable Templates for opening/closing checklists, SOPs, shift routines, maintenance tasks, or recurring operations.
  • Shared Shift & Task Calendar: Visualize all tasks and shifts in a clean calendar view. Instantly see who is working, what is due, and what’s overdue.

Perfect For

Retail & Hospitality teams, Cleaning, HVAC, and maintenance crews, Logistics & field operations.

Sustainmetrics: Carbon Footprint Tool

Clean Design. Fast Interaction. Accurate Results.

Sustainmetrics is a lightweight, browser-based carbon accounting tool designed to simplify emission calculations for organizations, sustainability teams, and consultants. It runs entirely in your browser for maximum security and speed.

What We Built

  • Multi-Source Emission Factors: Integrated factors from Defra, EPA, and ADEME, allowing users to calculate Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions using reliable global datasets.
  • Instant Carbon Inventory: As users add activities, the system automatically updates total emissions. Includes contextual guidance panels for data accuracy.
  • Save, Load & Excel Export: Users can save their entries, reload past inventories, and export carbon results directly to Excel for reporting. No login required.

Who It’s For

Sustainability professionals, environmental engineers, hotel & tourism operations, and consultants preparing carbon reports.
