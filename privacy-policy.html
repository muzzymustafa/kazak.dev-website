Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 03, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how Taskative ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information about users ("you," "user") of the application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information
- Email Address: Collected during registration to facilitate authentication and account management.

2.2 Task and Group Information
We collect data on tasks you create, groups you join, and your interactions within these groups.

2.3 Device Information
- Device Type and OS: Collected to improve application performance and resolve technical issues.
- Log Data: Error reports and performance data may be gathered during app usage.

2.4 Notifications
- Notification Token: Used to send push notifications to your device.

3. Use of Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:
- Service Provision: To provide core functionality of the app.
- Communication: To inform you about important account-related updates.
- Improvement: To enhance the app’s performance and user experience.
- Security: To prevent fraud and ensure app security.

4. Information Sharing

Your personal data is not shared with third parties, except in the following cases:
- Legal Obligations: To comply with legal requirements or respond to lawful requests.
- Service Providers: We use trusted third-party service providers, such as Firebase by Google, to support essential functionalities of our app. Firebase helps us with data storage, authentication, and notifications. These providers process data strictly for operational purposes, under strict confidentiality agreements, and in compliance with data protection regulations.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored on servers outside your country of residence. By using our app, you consent to such cross-border data transfers.

8. User Rights

Access and Correction: You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you. Upon request, we will provide you with a copy of your data and, where possible, details on how it is used. If you believe that any of the information we hold is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request corrections.

Deletion Request: You have the right to request the deletion of your account and any associated personal data. To initiate this process, navigate to the "Account and Data Deletion" section in the app settings. Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems within [e.g., 30 days].

Objection: You may object to specific data processing activities that you believe are not necessary for the operation of the app or that you do not wish to consent to, especially if these activities involve direct marketing or data profiling.

We currently do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies. If this changes, we will update this policy accordingly.
